---
title: "How do I switch from test mode to taking real card payments?"
category: "Payments"
---

When you first set up your club, card payments are in **test mode**. That means you (and the board) can practice without real money moving. When you're ready for members to pay real dues, you flip the switch to **live mode**.

### Before you go live

Check these three things — most boards trip on at least one:

1. **Your bank account is connected.** Money goes there when members pay.
2. **Your business name is verified.** Stripe (our card processor) needs to confirm your club is real.
3. **You've done at least one test payment.** Run a $1 charge against your own card on test mode to make sure the flow works.

### Flipping the switch

1. Go to **Settings → Payments**.
2. Find the **Card payments** section.
3. You'll see a yellow banner that says "Test mode — no real money is moving."
4. Click **Go live**.
5. You'll be sent through a quick verification with our card processor (Stripe). You'll need:
   - Your club's tax ID (EIN) or your personal SSN if you're a sole proprietor
   - The board's bank account routing and account numbers
   - A board member's name, address, and date of birth as the "responsible person"
6. After verification (usually instant, sometimes a day), the banner turns green.

### What happens after I go live?

- Members paying through your club app are charged real money.
- Funds land in your bank account in 2-3 business days.
- You'll see real payments on the **Payments** page with a "card" pill.

### What if I'm not ready?

That's fine. Stay in test mode. You can still record cash and check payments manually under **Payments → Mark paid**. Many small clubs run an entire season on cash and checks alone.

> **Tip:** Don't go live during dues week. Pick a slow week so you have time to handle questions if anything looks unfamiliar.
