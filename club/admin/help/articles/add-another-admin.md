---
title: "How do I add another admin (treasurer, secretary) to my account?"
category: "Getting Started"
---

Every club should have at least **two admins**. If only one person can sign in and that person goes on vacation or loses their phone, the whole board is stuck. It only takes a minute to add a backup.

### Adding a new admin

1. Click **Settings** in the top nav.
2. Scroll down to the **Club admins** card.
3. Under **Invite a co-admin**, fill in:
   - **Their name** (e.g. "Jane Smith")
   - **Their email** (this is what they'll use to sign in)
   - **Role** — pick from Membership Chair, Treasurer, Events / Programs, Communications / Marketing, or Owner / President (full access)
   - **Phone** (optional, used for SMS login when your club has Twilio set up)
4. (Optional) Open **Customize what they can see** to fine-tune which tabs they have access to. Most clubs leave this on the role default.
5. Click **Send invite**.

The new admin gets an email with a temporary password and a sign-in link. Once they sign in, they can change their password under their own account.

### What can each role do?

- **Owner / President** — full access, including inviting other admins. Every club needs at least one.
- **Treasurer** — payments, dues, and member rosters.
- **Membership Chair** — applications, households, and renewals.
- **Events / Programs** — calendar, swim lessons, parties.
- **Communications / Marketing** — announcements, campaigns, photos.

Only Owners can invite, remove, or change another admin's role.

### Removing an admin

When someone steps off the board:

1. Go to **Settings → Club admins**.
2. Find their name in the list.
3. Click the red **Remove** button on their row.
4. Confirm.

Their sign-in stops working right away. Their past actions stay in the activity log, so you can always see who did what. (You can also click **Change role** to demote them instead, or **Reset PW** if they forget their password.)

### What if I forget to add a backup and lose my own access?

Email support@poolsideapp.com from the email address listed on file at your club. We'll verify and get you back in. But please add a backup admin today — it's the single best 30-second insurance policy for your club.

> **Tip:** Use your club's main email account (e.g. `pool@yourclub.org`) as one of the Owner accounts so the role passes naturally to next year's board.
