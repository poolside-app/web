---
title: "How do I add another admin (treasurer, secretary) to my account?"
category: "Getting Started"
---

Every club should have at least **two admins**. If only one person can sign in and that person goes on vacation or loses their phone, the whole board is stuck. It only takes a minute to add a backup.

### Adding a new admin

1. Go to **Settings** in the top nav.
2. Scroll to the **Admin team** section.
3. Click **Add admin**.
4. Fill in their:
   - First and last name
   - Email address (this is what they'll sign in with)
   - Role — pick from Treasurer, Secretary, Membership, President, or Other
5. Click **Send invite**.

The new admin gets an email with a sign-in link. Once they click it, they're in.

### What can each admin do?

Right now, every admin has the same access — they can see everything and change everything. We're working on per-role permissions for a future update (so a Membership Chair can't accidentally edit payments). For now, only invite people you trust with the full club account.

### Removing an admin

Boards turn over every year. When someone steps off the board:

1. Go to **Settings → Admin team**.
2. Click the **×** next to their name.
3. Confirm.

Their sign-in stops working immediately. Their past actions stay in the audit log so you can always see who did what.

### What if I forget to add a backup and lose my own access?

Email support@poolsideapp.com from the email address listed on file at your club. We'll verify and get you back in. But please add a backup admin today — it's the single best 30-second insurance policy for your club.

> **Tip:** Add your club's main email account (e.g. `pool@yourclub.org`) as one admin so the role passes naturally to next year's board.
