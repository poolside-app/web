---
title: "How do I refund a payment?"
category: "Payments"
---

Refunds aren't done inside Poolside — they're done in your Stripe dashboard, because the money has to come from the same place it landed.

### If the payment was a card payment (Stripe)

1. Go to [dashboard.stripe.com](https://dashboard.stripe.com) and sign in with the email used when you connected Stripe.
2. Click **Payments** in the left sidebar.
3. Find the charge by member name, amount, or date.
4. Click into it and click **Refund**.
5. Choose full or partial, add a reason if you like, and confirm.

The money lands back on the member's card in 5-10 business days. Stripe automatically pulls our 1.5% fee back too.

After the refund posts in Stripe, **come back to Poolside → Payments**, find the household's row, and either:
- Click **Mark paid** off (if there's a way to undo on that row), or
- Email support@poolsideapp.com and we'll re-open the balance for them.

> **Heads-up:** This is a known gap — we're working on a one-click refund button inside Poolside. For now, the Stripe dashboard is the source of truth.

### If the payment was a check or cash

There's nothing electronic to undo — just write them a check (or hand back the cash) and remove the paid status:

1. Email support@poolsideapp.com with the household name and amount, and we'll mark it unpaid for you.

### What about Venmo or PayPal?

Same — refund inside Venmo/PayPal first, then ask us to flip the status back to unpaid in Poolside.

### Can I just delete the household?

If you want to fully remove the family (e.g. they joined by mistake), Stripe still needs the refund first or the money stays in your bank with no record. Always refund before removing.
