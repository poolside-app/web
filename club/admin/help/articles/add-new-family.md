---
title: "How do I add a new family to the club?"
category: "Members & Households"
---

There are two ways a family ends up in your member list — pick whichever fits the situation.

### The family is signing up themselves (recommended)

Most new families should fill out your **public application form**. The link is:

`https://<your-slug>.poolsideapp.com/apply.html`

Replace `<your-slug>` with your club's subdomain (you can see it on your **Dashboard** at the top — it shows under your club name, e.g. `bishopestates.poolsideapp.com`).

1. Copy that link and email it, post it on Facebook, or print it on a flyer with the QR code your members can scan.
2. They fill it in (name, address, kids, signatures).
3. Their submission shows up under **Members → Applications** as **pending**.
4. Approve it (see "How do I approve a membership application?") and they're in.

This is the easiest path because the family fills in their own contact info — no typos to chase down later.

### You're adding a family directly (the manual way)

Use this when a family paid in cash at the pool or you're entering legacy members during setup.

1. Click **Members** in the top nav.
2. Stay on the **Households** sub-tab (the default).
3. Click **+ Add household** at the top right.
4. Fill in the household's address, primary contact (name, email, phone), and tier.
5. Add each adult and child under the household.
6. Save.

The household will appear in your list immediately. Their dues status starts as **unpaid** until you record a payment, so don't forget to mark them paid (under **Payments**) if they already gave you a check.

> **Tip:** If you only have a phone number and no email, that's fine — you can still add them. They'll get text messages (via SMS sign-in) instead of emails when your club has Twilio set up.
