---
title: "How do I add a new family to the club?"
category: "Members & Households"
---

There are two ways a family ends up in your member list — pick whichever fits the situation.

### The family is signing up themselves (recommended)

Most new families should fill out your **membership application** form. It's the public link you can share by email or post on Facebook.

1. Go to **Settings** and copy the link under "Public application form."
2. Share it with the family.
3. They fill it in (name, address, kids, etc.) and you'll see their application show up under the **Members** tab.
4. Approve it (see the article on approving applications) and they're in.

This is the easiest path because the family fills in their own contact info — no typos.

### You're adding a family directly (the manual way)

Use this when a family paid in cash at the pool or you're entering legacy members during setup.

1. Go to **Members**.
2. Click **Add household**.
3. Fill in the household's last name and primary contact (phone + email).
4. Add each family member — adults first, then kids with birthdates.
5. Save.

The household will appear in your list immediately. Their dues status starts as **unpaid** until you record a payment, so don't forget to mark them paid if they already gave you a check.

> **Tip:** If you only have a phone number and no email, that's fine — you can still add them, and they'll get text messages instead of emails.
