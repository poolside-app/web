---
title: "How do I set or change dues amounts (membership tiers)?"
category: "Getting Started"
---

In Poolside, the prices your members pay live on something called a **tier**. A tier is just a level — like **Family** ($400), **Single** ($250), or **Senior** ($150). Most clubs have 2-4 tiers; some have just one.

### Setting them up the first time

1. Click **Members** in the top nav.
2. Click the **Tiers** sub-tab.
3. You'll see a list of any tiers already there. Click **+ Add tier** to add a new one.
4. For each tier, fill in:
   - **Name** — what families see when they apply (e.g. "Family", "Couple", "Single", "Senior 65+")
   - **Price** — the dollar amount for one season's dues
   - **Description** (optional) — anything members should know, like "Includes 2 adults + kids under 21"
5. Click **Save tiers** at the top.

The first tier in the list is the default — it's pre-selected on the application form.

### Changing prices for next season

1. Go to **Members → Tiers**.
2. Click into the price field on any tier and type the new amount.
3. Click **Save tiers**.

> **Heads-up:** Changing a price doesn't change what current members already owe. It only affects new applications and next year's renewals. To adjust an open charge for a current family, head to **Payments**.

### Don't have set prices? (per-household billing)

Some smaller clubs decide each family's dues by hand. That works in Poolside too:

1. Create one tier called something like "Standard" and leave the price blank (or $0).
2. After approving each family, go to their household record and add a charge under their name with whatever amount you decided.

### Want to offer an early-bird discount?

Don't change your tier prices — instead, set up an early-bird discount on the **Members → Renewals** page. That way the regular price is still on file, and the discount only applies if members pay before your deadline.
