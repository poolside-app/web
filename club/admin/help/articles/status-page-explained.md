---
title: "What does the Status page tell me?"
category: "Getting Started"
---

The **Status** page (in your top nav) is a one-stop health check for your club. Think of it as a dashboard that says, in plain English, "everything's fine" or "here's what to fix."

### The three sections

**Things to do** (top of page)

Red and yellow items that need your attention. Each one has a link or instructions to fix it. Tackle red items first — those mean something is broken for your members right now. Yellow items are improvements (like "you only have one admin account — add a backup").

**What members can do right now**

A list of everyday actions, each with a green check, yellow warning, or red X:

- Sign in with email
- Pay dues with a card
- Get emails
- Get text messages
- See the pool calendar
- Apply for membership

If everything is green here, your members are having a good experience. If anything is yellow or red, follow the link to fix it.

You can click **Send to me** next to email or text checks to send yourself a real test message — easier than asking a member to test for you.

**Behind the scenes** (collapsed by default)

The technical details — which outside services are hooked up, your web address status, the card processor, and so on. Most days you don't need to look here. Open it only when our support team asks you to.

### When should I check Status?

- **Once a week** during the off-season — quick peek to make sure nothing has drifted.
- **Every day** during dues season or after a big membership push.
- **Right away** if a member emails saying something doesn't work.

### The Email Support button

At the bottom there's a button to email Poolside support. It attaches a snapshot of everything on the Status page for you, so we can help without a long back-and-forth. Use it any time.
