---
title: "How do I send a renewal email or text blast?"
category: "Email & Text"
---

When dues season rolls around, you'll want to send everyone a reminder. Here's how to do it without forgetting anyone.

### Send a renewal email to everyone

1. Go to **Emails** in the top nav.
2. Click **New email**.
3. Pick the audience — usually **All households** or **Unpaid households only**.
4. Pick a template, or write your own. The "Renewal reminder" template is already written for you and includes a payment link.
5. Click **Preview** to see how it'll look — check it on your own phone if you can.
6. Click **Send**.

Sent emails show up in **Emails** with a delivery count. If 95 of 100 went through, the other 5 had bad email addresses or full inboxes — you can see exactly who.

### Send a text blast instead

Texts get higher open rates than email — most boards send a text blast a week before the deadline.

1. Go to **Emails** and switch to the **Text** tab.
2. Pick your audience (same options as email).
3. Keep it short — under 160 characters is best.
4. Include the payment link.
5. Send.

### Targeting only the people who haven't paid yet

This is the most common ask. On either the email or text screen, choose audience = **Unpaid households**. The list updates automatically as people pay, so you won't accidentally pester someone who already wrote you a check.

> **Important:** Texts are limited each month based on your plan. Don't worry — important reminders to existing members never count against the cap. Only marketing-style blasts do.
