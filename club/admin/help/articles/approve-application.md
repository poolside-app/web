---
title: "How do I approve a membership application?"
category: "Members & Households"
---

When a family fills out your public application form, it shows up as **pending** until you review it. Here's how.

1. Go to **Members** and look at the top — pending applications are listed first.
2. Click the family's name to open the application.
3. Read through their info. Common things to double-check:
   - Are the kids listed with the right ages?
   - Did they include a phone number?
   - Does their address match a real street in your neighborhood (if you only allow nearby residents)?
4. Click **Approve** to add them to the club, or **Decline** if there's a problem.

After you approve them, two things happen automatically:

- They get a **welcome email** with a sign-in link.
- They appear in your main member list.

### What if I need to ask them a question first?

Click **Email applicant** on the application page. It opens your email app with their address pre-filled. Once they reply, you can come back and approve.

### What if I approved by mistake?

Don't worry — go to the household's record under Members and click **Remove household**. They're hidden, not erased. You can bring them back later if you need to.

> **Tip:** Most boards approve applications once a week. Set a reminder on your phone for Sunday evenings.
