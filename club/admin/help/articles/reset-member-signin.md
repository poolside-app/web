---
title: "How do I reset a member's sign-in?"
category: "Members & Households"
---

Members sign in to your club app using a **magic link**. They type their email, and a sign-in link arrives in their inbox. There's no password to forget, so there's not much to "reset."

But sometimes a member calls and says "I can't get in." Here's the fix.

### Step 1: Check the email on file

1. Go to **Members** and find the household.
2. Confirm the email matches what the member is typing on the sign-in page.
3. If it's wrong, fix it and save.

### Step 2: Resend the sign-in link

Click **Resend sign-in link** on the household page. This sends a fresh magic link to the email on file. Tell the member to:

- Check spam if it doesn't arrive in 1-2 minutes.
- Open the link on the same device they want to be signed in on.
- Click the link within 15 minutes — they expire.

### Step 3: If they still can't get in

The most common cause is that they're typing one email but the household record has a different one (e.g. they signed up with `mom@aol.com` but they're typing `mom@gmail.com`).

Look closely at both. If they want to switch to a new email, update it on the household page and resend.

### What about kids and other family members?

Kids don't sign in separately — the parent's account covers the whole household. If two adults in the household want their own sign-in, add a second adult contact under the household record and they'll each get their own link.

> **Tip:** Magic links are safer than passwords. Members can never "forget" them — they just request a new one.
