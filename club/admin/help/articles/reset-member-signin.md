---
title: "How do I help a member who can't sign in?"
category: "Members & Households"
---

Members sign in to your club app using a **magic link**. They type their email or phone number on your member sign-in page, and a sign-in link arrives in their inbox or as a text. There's no password to forget, so there's not much to "reset."

But sometimes a member calls and says "I can't get in." Here's the fix.

### Step 1: Confirm the email or phone on file

1. Click **Members** in the top nav.
2. Find the household and click their row.
3. Compare the email and phone listed there with what the member is typing.

If the email or phone on file is wrong:

1. Fix it.
2. Save.
3. Tell the member to go back to your member sign-in page and try again — `https://<your-slug>.poolsideapp.com/m/`

### Step 2: Tell them how to request a fresh link

Send them this short instruction:

> "Go to **<your-slug>.poolsideapp.com/m/**, type your email (or phone), and click **Send link**. The link arrives in 1-2 minutes — check spam if it doesn't show up. Open the link on the device you want to be signed in on. Links work for 15 minutes."

### Step 3: If they still can't get in

The most common cause is that they're typing one email but the household record has a different one (e.g. they signed up with `mom@aol.com` but they're typing `mom@gmail.com`). Look closely at both. If they want to switch to a new email, update it on their household record and tell them to try again with the new one.

### What about kids and other family members?

Kids don't sign in separately — the parent's account covers the whole household. If two adults in the household want their own sign-in, both adult contacts in the household record need their own email or phone. Each one can then request their own magic link.

> **Tip:** Magic links are safer than passwords. Members can't "forget" them — they just request a new one.
