---
title: "How do I customize the welcome email?"
category: "Email & Text"
---

The welcome email is what new members see first when you approve their application. It sets the tone — a warm, specific greeting beats a generic "Welcome!" every time.

### Where to find it

1. Go to **Emails** in the top nav.
2. Click the **Templates** tab.
3. Find **Welcome email** in the list.
4. Click **Edit**.

### What you can change

- **Subject line** — keep it short. "Welcome to [Your Club]!" works well.
- **Greeting** — `Hi {{first_name}},` will fill in the new member's first name automatically.
- **Body** — write a few short paragraphs. Cover:
  - When the pool opens and closes
  - Where to find the gate code or app
  - Who to email with questions
  - Any rules they should know upfront (no glass, etc.)
- **Sign-off** — "Welcome aboard, [Your Name], Membership Chair" feels personal.

### The placeholders (those `{{double curly braces}}`)

These get filled in automatically for each member:

- `{{first_name}}` — the new member's first name
- `{{club_name}}` — your club's name
- `{{signin_link}}` — their personal sign-in link

Don't remove the sign-in link — it's how they get into the app.

### Test it before going live

Click **Send test to me** at the top of the editor. The email arrives in your inbox in a few seconds. Read it carefully — typos in welcome emails look unprofessional.

> **Tip:** Boards that personalize this email see fewer "I can't sign in" calls because the instructions are clearer.
